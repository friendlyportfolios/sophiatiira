Hey you,
I’m happy you stopped by!

I’m Sofia, a UX Writer from Finland that was repotted in Amsterdam in May 2017. Currently, I’m flinging pixels and solving problems at Booking.com. Over the last 6 years experience I’ve accumulated a range of skills as multidisciplinary designer.

The projects that get me most excited are ones where I get to dabble in the whole process from kick off all the way through to toasting champaign after launch! The only thing I love more than designing solutions, is see those solutions ship.

I enjoy working closely with the whole team including copywriters, researchers, and engineers. I think it’s really important to get everyone involved in the beginning to set out mission for a project and determine what steps we need to take to get there. That way when we reach that finish line, we can all take ownership of the badass products we shipped.

If you are keen on working together, take a peep at my recent work and add me on LinkedIn. I hope to hear from you soon!
